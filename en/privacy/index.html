Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Chinese Almanac is designed as a local-only app. It does not require an account, does not use a backend service, and does not actively send your app data to us.

Data Stored On Your Device

The app may store saved plans, notes, selected dates, scores, and language preference locally on your device using iOS local storage.

Data Collection

We do not collect personal data, analytics, advertising identifiers, location, contacts, photos, health data, or payment data.

Network Use

Chinese Almanac does not actively make network requests during normal app use. The app opens this privacy page or the support page only when you tap those links.

Tracking

Chinese Almanac does not track you across apps or websites and does not include third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.
